About BrightMind Ethiopia

BrightMind Ethiopia is a professional mental health and wellbeing platform dedicated to improving emotional, psychological, and social wellbeing.

We provide mental health education, counseling support, family guidance, relationship support, student counseling, and personal growth resources.

Our mission is to reduce stigma, promote awareness, and empower individuals to build healthier minds and stronger relationships.

Our Vision & Mission

🌍 Vision

To create a mentally healthier Ethiopia where individuals, families, and communities can thrive emotionally, socially, and psychologically.

🎯 Mission

To provide accessible mental health education, counseling support, emotional guidance, and personal development resources.

Founder Profile

Dawit Abera

Psychiatric Professional | Mental Health Advocate | Founder of BrightMind Ethiopia

BrightMind Ethiopia was created to provide accessible mental health awareness, counseling guidance, and emotional support for individuals, students, families, and communities.

The platform combines evidence-based psychological knowledge with compassionate support to promote resilience, wellbeing, and personal development.

"Seeking support is not a sign of weakness. It is a step toward growth, healing, and resilience."
